Key Objectives Achieved: Our solution aimed to (1) make Spotify Premium the #1 choice for students, (2) make discovery feel effortless and personalized, and (3) strengthen Spotify's presence in student life‚Äîwith measurable goals including a 5% increase in personalized playlist interaction and a 3% boost in social discovery features.

Key Learnings & Next Steps

While testing showed strong interest, we also received valuable feedback about improving feature differentiation and addressing privacy concerns around campus data sharing.

Future Considerations: Conduct deeper research on paywall models and privacy frameworks, explore student-controlled visibility toggles, and ensure alignment with Spotify's native UI patterns and backend constraints.

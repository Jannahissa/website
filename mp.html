MileagePlus Dashboard

Converting Competitors to Loyalty Members
Role: Product Designer
Timeline: Summer 2025
Company: United Airlines

Overview

As part of United's Product Design team, I designed and developed a digital dashboard for the "Frequent Flyer Switchers" campaign. The goal was to convert loyal customers from competing airlines (Delta, American) to MileagePlus by showcasing personalized benefits and rewards.

The dashboard integrated behavioral design principles and data-driven insights to create compelling conversion experiences that drove engagement and loyalty program sign-ups.

Design Process

1

Research & Discovery

Analyzed competitor loyalty programs, conducted user interviews with frequent flyers, and identified key pain points in the switching process.

2

Ideation & Wireframing

Created low-fidelity wireframes exploring different dashboard layouts, tested information hierarchy, and refined user flows based on stakeholder feedback.

3

Design & Prototyping

Developed high-fidelity mockups in Figma, created interactive prototypes, and collaborated with the creative team on brand consistency.

4

Testing & Iteration

Conducted A/B testing on key conversion points, gathered analytics data, and refined the design based on user behavior patterns.

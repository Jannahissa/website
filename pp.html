Peer Pressure

Designing positive peer accountability for better digital habits
Role: Lead UI Designer
Platform: Mobile App

Overview

Peer Pressure is a behavioral design app that uses social accountability to help users manage screen time, reduce distractions, and build healthier digital habits. The platform leverages positive peer influence and habit tracking to create meaningful change through community support.

My Role

As Lead UI Designer, I designed and prototyped a fully responsive interface in Figma — from low-fidelity wireframes to high-fidelity mockups and interactive prototypes. My responsibilities included:

🎨
Designing and prototyping a responsive UI in Figma for habit tracking, peer accountability, and distraction management.
🎯
Developing a cohesive brand kit — including the logo, color palette, typography, and UI components — to ensure strong visual identity and recognition.
👥
Conducting user research through interviews and usability testing to refine navigation flows, accessibility, and retention.
🔧
Building a scalable design system with reusable components and interaction guidelines, collaborating closely with developers for pixel-perfect implementation.

Design Process

The design process began with user interviews focused on understanding how peer dynamics influence productivity and screen habits. Insights revealed that users were more likely to stay accountable when progress and encouragement were shared socially.

From there, I developed journey maps and wireframes to visualize the app's flow — from onboarding to habit tracking — followed by iterative usability tests to refine the experience. The final prototype emphasizes simplicity, clarity, and motivational design.

Key Features

🤝
Peer Coaching: Connect with friends who provide encouragement when you exceed your screen limits.
📱
Screen Time Tracking: Monitor app usage and set healthy limits across apps and categories.
📊
Usage Analytics: Gain insights into behavior patterns to make meaningful improvements.
🔔
Peer Notifications: Your accountability partners get notified when thresholds are reached.
🎯
Progress Tracking: Celebrate milestones and habit improvements over time.

Impact

Through design iteration and user validation, Peer Pressure achieved strong usability and engagement metrics in testing, helping users reduce their average screen time by up to 20% while increasing motivation through shared accountability. The app's social-first approach sets it apart from typical habit trackers by transforming self-control into a community-driven experience.
